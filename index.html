Values

Human-first. AI-transparent. Anti-sludge.

I use AI to learn, organize, and prototype. But the goal is not to replace artists, developers, educators, testers, or specialists.

I want real craft, real credit, honest expectations, and fairer money conversations as projects grow.

Collaboration & Use

This is a public project garden, not a free-for-all. Please do not present my project names, writing, prototypes, designs, code, or specific concepts as your own.

If something sparks an idea, reach out. I would rather build with people than lock everything away.
